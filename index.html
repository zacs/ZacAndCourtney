Zac was born in the Washington DC area, and quickly moved to the Philly area until he was four, which is when he moved to the dirty south (also known as Georgia). From that young age, Zac has always been interested in the wilderness and in technology, and started playing basketball with his dad when he was only 5. It isn't known by many, but Zac has actually summited the 7 highest peaks in the world, while wearing nothing but a freshly pressed suit. Class, elegance, and sophistication have always been important to him.


Courtney was born and raised in Chicago, and thus, has a unique appreciation for delicious steaks. Unfortunately, she is now in a city where red meat is unheard of—fortunately, Seattle's seafood makes up for it. After growing up a basketball and volleyball player, Courtney got prissy and decided to focus on dance in high school. She has always loved to dance, and ended up with a cadre of bridesmaids who were all on the high school dance team with her (except you, Lauren and Taylor, sorry).

The two met at the University of Illinois in the fall semester of 2005. They were brought together by the fact that they are both incredibly passionate individuals, who care about nothing but providing for others. Either that, or they both joined a community service fraternity to put it on their resumés. You should be able to guess which.

Soon after meeting, Zac used his superb middle school-style flirting techniques to woo Courtney. For the last few years of college, they shared Illini basketball season tickets, frequented Murphy's Pub (usually playing classic rock on the jukebox), and somehow managed to spend about 20 hours per day together, despite having class and separate apartments.

After finishing school, Zac moved to Seattle to work for Microsoft, and Courtney joined him just a few months later, soon finding her first gig at the American Heart Association. While Courtney has moved on to work at Redfin since then, Zac is still at Microsoft working on strange cloud computing things you've likely not heard of.

Zac proposed to Courtney on March 23rd, while the two were drinking some IPA and having a relaxed evening. Courtney incredulously replied with "Really?", which wasn't the first thing Zac wanted to hear, but she followed with a quick "Yes!"
